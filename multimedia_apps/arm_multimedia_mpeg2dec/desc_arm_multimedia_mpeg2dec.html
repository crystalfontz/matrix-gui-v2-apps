ARM MPEG-2 Video Decode

This example demonstrates video content decoding capability of Cortex-A8 core using MPEG-2 codec. The Multimedia framework used is GStreamer and gst-ffmpeg plug-in is used to call into open source FFmpeg codec libraries.

Pipeline

gst-launch
 |_ mpegvideoparse
   |_ ffdec_mpeg2video
     |_ v4l2/fbdev sink
